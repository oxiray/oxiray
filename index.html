What does OxIRay do?

A smart wrist band with the ability to constantly monitor body temperature and SpO2 levels along with an inbuilt sanitizer mist spray apparatus apart from functioning like any other smart watch. The temperature and the pulse oximeter sensors record their respective values and that data is sent to the microcontroller which is connected via Wi-Fi. The device uploads the data to the database and is accessible through our mobile application where all the details of the person are stored and analysed

Salient features

In case of deteriorating health of an employee, mobile application sends notification to alert the employer to take necessary steps. Reduces the number of visits by a caregiver monitoring home isolated patients. Refillable ”sanitizer-on-the-go” thus preventing unnecessary contact with surfaces.







Edge over competition

We use both IR and visible red light to measure SpO2 with increased accuracy. Mobile application specifically designed to aid in mass screening.Comes along with an inbuilt refillable sanitizer mist spray apparatus.
